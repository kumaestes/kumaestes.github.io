No parking on city streets Nov. 1ST - March 31st

SNOW PLOWING SEASON IS HERE! Please remember the following to help the City with snow removal and be considerate of your neighbors. Parking of motor vehicles, trailers, snowmobiles or other objects on any city street is NOT permitted from November 1st to March 31st regardless of the amount of snow fall.

Violations of the winter parking rules are an infraction with a fine of $100 to $300 for each occurrence. Parking on Main Street in the business district is allowed during business hours. Private snow removal reminder: It is unlawful to deposit snow on any private property or public street or sidewalk.

Snow is required to be placed on your own property. Violation of this is a misdemeanor. The City of Soda Springs greatly appreciates the cooperation of all City Residents. Thank you and have a safe and happy winter.
